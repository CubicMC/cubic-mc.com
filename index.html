About Cubic Server

CubicServer is part of the game world as the Minecraft server creation solution for individuals and businesses. It is an ambitious project that promises its users performance and ease of use, through its simplified and optimized technical environment, but also through its extensibility through a plugin system.

Carried out by six Epitech students as a graduation project, the goal of CubicServer will be to offer an optimized server without limits, to allow players around the world to give free rein to their imagination within a expanded community.
